One day in the morning my mother bake a cake in the kitchen and my brother become his room. His bicycle become near the door. The Arab past near my house and looked on the bicycle. He come to my garden, take the bicycle and start run fast. My neighbor listen her got and he looked in the window out. He looked the Arab and go out very fast. My mother look the Arab but she does not who is. She went out and looked the Arab. She went to phone to the policeman and when they come my dog start to cry. The Arab listen the dog and start scared and he cry too. The policeman free the dog and it run very fast to Arab and want to bite him. The policeman give the Arab to the police station and the dog run very fast after them.
