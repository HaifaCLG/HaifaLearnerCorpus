Well, This picture describe a day in life some poor vilage has called Goldshine. this is the park in Goldshine and you can see the school and the football cort near and the grass when all the student like to smoke and get drunk. Left to the sechool we can see Dana every day tehre. Everybodyy are calling her FRIKIT all the tim because she is always alone and she talking to herself. There are two kids play basketball.
