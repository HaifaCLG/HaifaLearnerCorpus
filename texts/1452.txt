There are several possible ways for using the internet in language teaching.


First, the internet can be a great and efficient source for teachers to find new ideas for lesson plans, or for different activities, for their students.

Second, internet can be used by students as they write their essays in a special site, as a discussion forum. In this way teachers make sure all their students participate in the class discussion, also it would be beneficial for students to have a break from the close doors classroom routine.

Third way for using the internet in language teaching is, downloading songs or poems to teach the students, and make the lesson more interesting.

Thus, internet usage has to be more common and used in language teaching lessons in schools.
