Pesah
Me and my family always to pesah at our home but this year we did it with our ant in her suns home, thay live in Rishon lezion, we drove there 5 houres because all of the graffic. when we got there no body wore there except the dog. we called to my ant shopie, she said that shell be there in a few mints, she came, her husband, her two sons and her son wife, the parents of the wife came to. we sat and started to read Hagada, and when we finished it we started to tell jokes about pesah, than we ate, because its the pesah, there were nothing to eat, we sat there for enother cuple of houres and drove home.
