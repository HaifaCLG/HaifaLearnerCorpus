A murder
Several years ago I met Sherlock Holmes.
I thought he was a detective, as everyone thought, but I was wrong...
A month ago, my best friend disappeared. We thought he was kidnaped, but his body was found 3 days later. Then, his brother was lost, and was killed too. I decided to go to Mr. Holmes to ask for help, but when I entered his house, I saw there were many bodies of my friends, and I saw Holmes, coming towards me, holding a knife...
